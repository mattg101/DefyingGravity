# Feature: Exporting Blueprints

Once your design is complete, FrameTamer provides high-fidelity outputs for fabrication.

## 1. Mat Blueprint (PDF)
A 1:1 scale vector PDF showing cut lines and dimensions. Perfect for manual cutting or digital mat cutters.

## 2. Final Preview (JPEG)
A rendered image of your project, including the frame texture and shadows. Ideal for client approval or social media.

## 3. Metric Summary
All dimensions are listed in the MetricCard for quick reference during assembly.
